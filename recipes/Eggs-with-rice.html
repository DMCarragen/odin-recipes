Simple Diego Style Egg Fried Rice

It is easy to cook and delicious

Ingredients Needed

  • 4 to 6 eggs depending on servings wanted
  • a common coffee mug filled with rice
  • At least one kind of cheese, i suggest using grated cheese and mozzarella
  • 20 grams of butter
  • Milk Cream
  • Ciboulette

How To Make

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Pour the butter on a pan and after it slightly melts broke all the eggs and pour them
  3. broke the yolke and mix it with the white
  4. when the eggs are done mix it with the rice and add all the other ingredients (Milk Cream, all the cheeses, and the ciboulette)
  5. Fry the mix on the pan until the rice turns brownish.
