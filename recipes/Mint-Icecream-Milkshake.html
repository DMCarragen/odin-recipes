Mint Ice Cream Milkshake

It is just a mix of mint ice cream and milk with some other things, its pretty easy to make.

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 Pint of Mint or Choclate Chip Mint Ice Cream
  • Whole Milk (At least 1 mug of milk
  • Milk Cream
  • Oreos are Optional

How To Make

  1. Pour the Ice cream inside your blender
  2. Pour the milk (Quantity depends on your taste)
  3. Blend for about 10 seconds
  4. Add 1/4 pint of Milk Cream
  5. Blend some more
  6. Serve and if you like it put some oreos on top of your shake
